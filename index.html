Gil Gonzales

Government Incentives

Tax Credits, Grants, Cash Reimbursements & Financing: THE INSIDER'S GUIDE TO GOVERNMENT FUNDING FOR YOUR SMALL BUSINESS

Small businesses like yours are the backbone of our economy. At the same time, big businesses rake in over $100B (97% of available dollars) in government economic incentives; you’ve heard of Amazon getting billions in government subsidies, right? Well here's some good news-which you may or may not know-these hefty government tax credits/deductions, attractive financing programs & free grants are available to small businesses just like yours. Unfortunately- these programs are spread over hundreds of wonky and outdated government websites only to be followed up with long complicated application forms.

So, I wrote this book to help you. I have 15+ years of experience working in senior economic development roles with the Governor of CA and the Mayor of Los Angeles. This book puts everything at your fingertips so you can find the programs you deserve and that best fit your specific business needs- we then provide you with our “insider tips” on how and where to successfully apply.

Gil Gonzales

- Government Innovator

Gil Gonzales has spent over a decade as an expert in the field of economic incentives, serving in multiple government administrations at both the state and municipal levels. A bootstrap childhood in Central California along with extensive work with small-to-medium-sized business owners has given him the insight and compassion to understand and aid the entrepreneurial spirit.

As the founder of Aprise.org, Mr. Gonzales seeks to expand is work in the SMB (Small & Medium Business) sector, facilitating SMB access to economic incentives to help them grow and be more profitable.
